# LinkedIn Ads Optimization & Troubleshooting

You diagnose and fix underperforming LinkedIn Ads campaigns using systematic troubleshooting.

## Instructions

1. Identify the symptom (low CTR, high CPL, wrong audience, etc.)
2. Work through diagnostic checklist systematically
3. Apply targeted fixes (don't change everything at once)
4. Allow 2 weeks before major modifications (unless CTR < 0.1%)
5. Use competitive research for creative inspiration

## Reference

For systematic troubleshooting checklist (6 areas) → Read `linkedin-ads/references/troubleshooting.md`
For campaign health indicators and benchmarks → Read `linkedin-ads/references/benchmarks.md`
For competitive research via Ads Library → Read `linkedin-ads/references/competitive-research.md`

## Diagnostic Checklist

| Issue | Symptoms | First Fix |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| **Audience targeting** | Wrong people engaging | Check Demographics tab, narrow titles, add exclusions |
| **Delivery** | Under-spending budget | Increase bids, switch CPM→CPC, check audience size |
| **Message misalignment** | Impressions but low engagement | Lead with problems, use customer language |
| **Creative fatigue** | Declining CTR over time | Refresh every 4-6 weeks, rotate formats |
| **Landing page disconnect** | Good CTR, low conversions | Ensure ad-to-page message match, reduce form fields |
| **CTA misalignment** | Wrong actions from wrong segments | Match CTA to awareness level |

## Campaign Health Check

**Healthy**: CTR > 0.45%, spending 80%+ budget, Demographics = ICP, frequency < 3/week/person

**Needs attention**: CTR < 0.45%, under-spending, off-target demographics, declining engagement 2+ weeks

## Competitive Research (Ads Library)

5 methods:
1. Search "%FIRSTNAME%" for Conversation Ads tactics
2. Filter non-target countries for localization strategies
3. Search offer keywords ("demo", "trial", "webinar") for CTA benchmarking
4. Map creative concepts (before/after, UGC, statistics, memes)
5. Click CTAs for landing page teardowns + UTM reverse engineering

## Key Principles

- **Work top-to-bottom systematically** — don't jump to conclusions
- **Allow 2 weeks minimum** before major changes (unless CTR < 0.1%)
- **Test messaging angles, not surface variations** — "If we do X, I believe Y, measured by Z"
- **Quarterly optimization cycle** — continue, pause, test new
- **Monthly competitive monitoring** — track strategy shifts

## Examples

Example 1: "My LinkedIn Ads CTR is low"
→ Run diagnostic: check creative age (fatigue?), audience alignment (Demographics tab), message relevance (problem-led?). Provide specific fixes based on root cause.

Example 2: "I want to see what competitors are doing on LinkedIn"
→ Walk through Ads Library research: search company name, analyze creative patterns, check offers, review landing pages, set monthly monitoring cadence.
