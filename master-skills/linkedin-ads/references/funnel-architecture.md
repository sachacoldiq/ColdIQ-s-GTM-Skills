# Funnel Architecture

## Three-Layer Funnel

### Top of Funnel (Cold)
- **Objective**: Awareness, curiosity
- **Content**: Humor around pain points, educational content, thought leadership
- **Ad formats**: Video, Single Image, Thought Leader Ads, Document Ads
- **KPIs**: Impressions, engagement rate, cost per engagement, video views
- **Budget**: ~50% of total
- **Key**: "You're showing up unannounced in someone's feed—simply pique curiosity"

### Middle of Funnel (Warm)
- **Objective**: Trust-building, consideration
- **Content**: Product videos, testimonials, case studies, webinar replays
- **Ad formats**: Carousel, Video, Document Ads, Thought Leader Ads
- **KPIs**: CTR, engaged website visits, retargeting pool size
- **Budget**: ~25% of total

### Bottom of Funnel (Hot / Retargeting)
- **Objective**: Direct conversion (demo, trial, event)
- **Content**: Demo requests, pricing, trials, direct CTA
- **Ad formats**: Single Image, Lead Gen Forms, Conversation Ads, Message Ads
- **KPIs**: Cost per demo, cost per SQL, cost per opportunity, pipe-to-spend ratio
- **Budget**: ~25% of total

## Retargeting Windows

| Window | Audience | Content | Goal |
|--------|----------|---------|------|
| 30 days | Recent site visitors | Direct CTAs (demo, book call) | Convert in-market |
| 90 days | Considering prospects | Case studies, testimonials, thought leadership | Move to consideration |
| 180 days | Previous visitors, not in-market | Spotlight & Text Ads (low-cost) | Maintain awareness |

## Retargeting Segments to Build
- Website visitors (by page: pricing, demo, blog, etc.)
- Company page visitors
- Video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%, 97%)
- Ad interactions / engagement
- Lead form opens (but not submitted)
- Event attendees
- Meeting no-shows and closed-lost opportunities

## Retargeting Creative Strategy
50/50 split:
- **50% value-add**: Thought leadership, social proof, product marketing
- **50% conversion**: Demos, trials, events, offers

## Pre-Launch Checklist
Before launching any campaign, outline:
1. Target audience availability on LinkedIn
2. Audience segment sizes
3. Budget constraints
4. Content inventory and gaps
5. Audience activity levels on platform
6. Clear KPIs per campaign type
7. Proper LinkedIn audience network settings
8. Strategic use of audience expansion and exclusions

## Optimization Cycle (Quarterly)
1. Identify what performed well → continue
2. Pause underperforming elements
3. Design new tests based on learnings
4. Allow ~$100 spend per creative before shutdown decisions

## Testing Framework
Formula: "If we do X, I believe Y, as measured by Z"

Meaningful tests:
- Different messaging angles (not surface-level creative variations)
- Native audiences vs ABM approaches
- Landing page variations with proper A/B structure
