# Ad Formats

## Single Image Ads
- **When**: Brand awareness, demand creation, lead capture
- **Specs**: Square (1200x1200) outperforms rectangular
- **Bidding**: Manual for cost control
- **Tip**: Use ICP-specific language, make ads blend into feed

## Video Ads
- **When**: Storytelling, product demos, testimonials, complex messaging
- **Specs**: Square (1080x1080), always add subtitles (auto-play muted)
- **Cost**: ~10-15c per view
- **Bidding**: CPV or Maximum Delivery
- **Track**: Video completion rates
- **Benefit**: Builds large retargeting pools

## Carousel Ads
- **When**: Feature showcase, use cases, customer stories
- **Tip**: Maintain consistent flow/narrative across all cards
- **Bidding**: Manual for engagement
- **Track**: CTR and engagement per card

## Thought Leader Ads
- **When**: Credibility, authority, expertise
- **Stats**: 63% higher CTR on mid-funnel content
- **Key insight**: Non-employee influencers often outperform employee content
- **Selection**: Posts with 2-3% organic CTR become top-performing ads
- **Bidding**: CPC or CPM for engagement/reach
- **Setup**: Sponsor via "browse existing content" in Campaign Manager
- **CTA**: Add link in pinned comment, use Premium CTAs if available

### 14 Thought Leader Ad Strategies
1. Product feature/integration announcements
2. Problem + Solution framework posts
3. Internal case studies (dog-fooding)
4. Customer case studies & success stories
5. Pre-event buzz (ABM targeting attendees)
6. Post-event recaps
7. Event promotion from speaker voices
8. Webinar promotion with clear value prop
9. Newsletter subscription promotion
10. Customer wins & awards celebration
11. Product use case demonstrations
12. Industry-specific thought leadership
13. Practical advice backed by platform data
14. Social proof from customer advocates

## Text Ads
- **When**: Website traffic, top-of-mind, cost-effective
- **Placement**: Right sidebar
- **Bidding**: Manual, keep bids low
- **Best for**: Low-cost remarketing complement

## Conversation Ads
- **When**: Lead gen, direct engagement, interactive journeys
- **Cost**: Billed per send (~$1 starting bid)
- **Tip**: Optimize subject lines, test incentivized offers
- **Search "%FIRSTNAME%" in Ads Library to find competitor examples

## Message Ads
- **When**: Direct outreach, personalized communication
- **Cost**: $1-2 starting bids per send
- **Tip**: Address pain points, use audience-appropriate tone, sender should resonate with audience

## Spotlight Ads
- **When**: Remarketing, top-of-mind awareness
- **Pros**: Lower CPM, high ad recall, personalized with viewer profile data
- **Bidding**: Manual, keep bids low

## Follower Ads
- **When**: LinkedIn page growth, organic reach expansion
- **Tip**: Target colder audiences to build organic nurture pool

## Document Ads
- **When**: Whitepapers, eBooks, case studies
- **Performance**: 6-7%+ CTR achievable
- **Tip**: Keep ungated for broader reach and retargeting pool building
- **Bidding**: Manual for leads and engagement

## Lead Gen Forms
- **When**: Demo bookings, event registrations, guide downloads
- **Tip**: Max 2-3 custom fields. Pre-filled data = high completion rates
- **Avoid**: "work mobile" or "work email" fields
- **Bidding**: Manual (start above minimum threshold)

## Connected TV Ads
- **When**: Hard-to-reach professional audiences, large-screen brand building

## Format Selection by Goal

| Goal | Recommended Formats |
|------|-------------------|
| Demand Capture | Single Image, Lead Gen Forms, Conversation, Message |
| Demand Creation | Single Image, Video, Thought Leader, Carousel |
| Accelerate Pipeline | Document, Carousel, Spotlight |
| Brand Awareness | Single Image, Video, Thought Leader, Text + Spotlight |
